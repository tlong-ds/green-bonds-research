# Policy Impact Analysis (ASEAN Green Bonds)

Evaluate the "Policy Implications for Policymakers" mentioned in the research outline (Page 14-15).

## Tasks

1. **Taxonomy Harmonization (H4)**:
   - Create dummy variables for the adoption of the ASEAN Taxonomy in different member states.
   - Test if adoption reduces the "yield penalty" for self-labeled bonds.
2. **Issuance Subsidy Evaluation**:
   - Analyze if countries with direct grants for SPO costs (e.g., Singapore, Malaysia) have higher issuance volumes or lower "greenium" barriers for mid-sized firms.
3. **Counterfactual Analysis**:
   - Model the potential growth in green bond volume if issuance costs were subsidized by X%.

## Output

Generate policy-brief style summaries and predictive charts showing the impact of regulatory interventions.
